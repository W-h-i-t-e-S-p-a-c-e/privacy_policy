Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sleep Cycle Alarm app ("App") handles your information. Your privacy is important, and this App is designed to collect as little information as possible.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Personal identifiers

Alarm and Sleep Data

Sleep cycle settings and alarm times are stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Internet Access

This App may use internet access only for basic functionality such as updates or displaying general information. It does not send personal data.

Third‑Party Services

This App does not use third‑party services that collect personal information.

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.

Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
